1.

Player throws a dice. Whenever a 6 is thrown, you may place a pawn onto the board. Otherwise you may move 1 pawn.

2.

Make a move (if you can.) Whenever your pawn would land on another's, you kill them and send them back to their home square.

3.

The goal is to get all 4 of your pawns to your street by walking them around the board. Goodluck and have fun!
